  1. Customer satisfaction and continuous customer satisfaction through early delivery.
  2. Welcome changing requirements, even in the late stages of project development. We should be good at using the change of requirements to help customers gain competitive advantage.
  3. Deliver available software over time, usually over a few weeks, as short as possible.
  4. Business people and developers must work together during the project.
  5. Projects must be built around individuals with intrinsic motivation, who should be trusted.
  6. Face to face conversation is the best way to communicate.
  7. Availability is the main indicator of progress.
  8. We should promote sustainable development and maintain a steady rate of progress.
  9. Constantly pay attention to whether the technology is excellent or not and whether the design is good.
  10. Simplicity is essential, minimizing unnecessary work.
  11. The best architecture, requirements and design come from spontaneous understanding within the team.
  12. The team should regularly reflect on how to be more effective and adjust accordingly.
